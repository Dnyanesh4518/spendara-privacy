Overview

Spendara ("we", "our", or "the app") is a personal finance companion app developed by Dnyanesh K, an independent developer based in India. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and the choices you have.

By downloading and using Spendara, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please uninstall the app.

The short version

Your transactions, goals, and financial data are stored only on your device using local storage. We cannot see them, access them, or share them. The only data that ever leaves your device is anonymous crash reports and advertising identifiers managed by Google — both of which you can opt out of.

Data We Do NOT Collect

We want to be explicit about what Spendara never collects or transmits:

  • Bank account numbers, UPI IDs, card numbers, or any payment credentials
  • Your contacts, SMS messages, or call logs
  • Your location (GPS or IP-based)
  • Your camera, microphone, or photos
  • Your transaction amounts, categories, or financial history
  • Your device files or storage contents
  • Your browsing history or other app usage
  • Any biometric data

Spendara does not require an internet connection to function. All core features — adding transactions, viewing insights, managing goals — work completely offline.

4

Local Data Storage

All your personal and financial data is stored locally on your Android device using Hive, a fast and lightweight local database. This data includes:

  • Your name and email address entered during onboarding
  • All transaction records you create (amount, type, category, date, notes)
  • All savings goals and deposit history
  • Your in-app preferences (language, theme)

🔐 Important: This data never leaves your phone. We have no server, no database, and no ability to access your financial information. If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is permanently deleted from your device.

If you use Android's built-in backup feature, your app data may be included in your Google account backup according to Google's own privacy policy. You can disable app backup in your Android settings.

5

Advertising — Google AdMob

Spendara uses Google AdMob to display banner advertisements. AdMob helps keep the app free for all users.

By using Spendara, you may be shown ads personalised to your interests by Google. AdMob may collect and use the following to deliver ads:

  • Google Advertising ID (GAID) — a resettable anonymous identifier
  • General device information (model, OS version)
  • Coarse IP-based location (country/region level only)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

⚡ Rewarded Ads: Spendara offers an optional "Remove Ads for 1 hour" feature. When you watch a rewarded ad voluntarily, the timestamp of your ad-free window is stored locally on your device only — never on any server.

Google's data practices for AdMob are governed by Google's Privacy Policy. You can review it at policies.google.com/privacy.

To opt out of personalised ads:

  • On your Android device, go to Settings → Google → Ads
  • Enable "Opt out of Ads Personalisation"
  • Or reset your Advertising ID from the same screen

Crash Reports & Analytics

Spendara uses crash reporting to identify and fix technical issues. When the app crashes or encounters an error, an anonymous report is sent that may include:

  • The type of error and which part of the app caused it
  • Your device model and Android version
  • App version number
  • Time and date of the crash

What crash reports never include: Your name, email, transaction data, financial information, or any personally identifiable content. Crash data is entirely anonymous and used solely to improve app stability.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through our app, please contact us at dnyanesh.k2002@gmail.com and we will promptly delete such information.

Your Rights & Choices

As a user of Spendara, you have full control over your data:

  • Access your data: All your data is visible within the app at all times — transactions, goals, and profile information.
  • Edit your data: You can edit or update any transaction, goal, or profile information directly in the app.
  • Delete your data: You can delete individual transactions, goals, or your entire profile from within the app. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all locally stored data.
  • Opt out of personalised ads: Follow the steps in Section 5 to reset or disable your advertising identifier.
  • Data portability: Your data is stored in a local Hive database on your device. You own it entirely.

Since all personal financial data is stored only on your device, we technically cannot access, export, or delete it on your behalf — you have complete control through the app itself.
